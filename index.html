What is the lead time calculator?
The lead time calculator is a tool that calculates the lead time of your supply delivery.
How to calculate lead time?
To calculate lead time you should use the following formula:
lead time = pre-production time + production time + post-production time
Why use lead time calculator?
The lead time calculator is used in order to measure the lead time of your supply delivery.
